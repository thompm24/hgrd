Adaptive Agronomy

At the heart of our business lies expert, in-field advice rooted in a profound agronomic understanding. Our dedicated team comprises local, highly skilled agronomists, certified with IASIS, BASIS, and FACTs qualifications. Working closely with farmers, we are committed to fostering the growth of profitable crops. Leveraging insights from independent trials programs and cutting-edge research from leading chemical manufacturers, our agronomists craft customized and cost-effective crop protection solutions tailored to local conditions

The three tiers of agronomy

  • Crop Protection
    • Collaborating with global chemical leaders and their technical teams, we prioritize timely delivery of essential chemicals to optimize crop health. Our agronomists receive daily electronic updates, ensuring they stay at the forefront of research and product advancements. Knowledge transfer to growers is our top priority.
  • Seed Selection
    • Understanding the interplay between crop varieties and their environment is crucial in the production process. Drawing from hands-on experience and trial briefings in Ireland and the UK, we provide expert advice on a wide range of major food crops, including cereals, potatoes, and vegetables.
  • Plant Nutrition
    • We understand the unique nutritional requirements of each crop and tailor a specialized nutrition program to meet those needs. Our independent Agronomists offer advice on all aspects of nutrition and crop establishment, ensuring you receive unbiased recommendations. Free from ties to fertilizer manufacturers, we provide comprehensive guidance to help maximize yields and safeguard margins.

Precision Farming

Used effectively, precision farming technology—encompassing yield mapping, automatic steering, and variable rate application—significantly enhances farm operation efficiency. The accurate application of fertilizers, both in terms of quantity and timing, plays a pivotal role in influencing yield, protein content, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact. Our collaboration with top precision farming application specialists positions Haggard Stores as a comprehensive 'one-stop' solution for the modern farmer. By leveraging these cutting-edge tools, farmers can fine-tune fertilization to meet the precise needs of plants at every point in the field, optimizing outcomes and sustainability.
