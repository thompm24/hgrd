Adaptive Agronomy

Agronomy is the science of soil management and the production of field crops.
In-field advice with an in-depth agronomic understanding is the cornerstone of our business. Our team of local, highly skilled IASIS, BASIS and FACTs qualified field agronomists work closely with their farmer customers to help them grow profitable crops. They use the results from various independent trials programs as well as research generated from the major chemical manufacturers to devise cost effective crop protection solutions tailored to meet local circumstances. Within our company we have 3 tiers of agronomy: Crop Protection, Seed Selection and Plant.
