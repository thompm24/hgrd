The Future of Fertilisers

Improving fertiliser efficiency is vital for sustainable food production, considering the growing population and environmental concerns. Haggard Stores prioritizes soil health and nutrient retention, promoting balanced crop nutrition and utilizing the 4R's principles. This approach enhances yields, profitability, and soil health while reducing the carbon footprint and environmental impact through innovative fertiliser strategies and sustainable agricultural practices like organic manures, conservation tillage, and cover crops.

P-Focus

Highly efficient liquid starter fertiliser promotes strong, early rooting with 100% protected phosphate, surpassing granular alternatives.

Efficient 28

Foliar nitrogen fertiliser, compatible with most plant protection products, ensures nearly 100% uptake with no scorching or leaching, unlike solid fertilisers.

BLADKALI-TS

Foliar Potassium & Sulphur that is crop (and soil) safe that can be applied tank-mixed with a spray programme
